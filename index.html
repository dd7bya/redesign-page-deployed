Belly Bombz Kitchen

a Korean-inspired concept

Voted "Best Wing Restaurant" 2016, 2017, 2018 at the Santa Anita Craft Beer and Wing Festival.

It started off as a playful conversation among a few friends. When Belly Bombz was first established, the food was served out of a food tent pitched up at a local farmer's market in Long Beach, CA. As the concept slowly started building a Sunday following, returning customers recommended Albert to look into opening a food truck.
